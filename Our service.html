Legal and Immigration Support:

 We support you in all things that are unfamiliar to you at your new place. Finding a general practitioner or visa application. We will guide you through the entire legal process of moving to your new country. Let us handle the complexities, while you focus on making the most of your expat journey.

Housing:

 Your new home far away from home. At Xpat we understand the difficulties of finding a place in a foreign country while you’re still at home. We're here to be your guides and your friendly neighbors. Helping you with every step of the way. 

After you created an account you are free to scroll for hours to find your perfect new spot. A house is not just about four walls and a roof, it is about creating a home, so take your time. Once you found some great options, simply contact us and we will take care of the rest, updating you with every news we have during the process.

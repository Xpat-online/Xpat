Testing Report

 

Testing Goals: 

The goal of testing our website was to evaluate the user interface and user experience to identify areas of improvement to enhance the website's functionality and increase user engagement. 

The specific testing goals are as follows: 

To determine if users can navigate the website with ease and accomplish tasks efficiently

 To identify any areas of confusion or difficulty during the user testing process 

To assess the effectiveness of the website design and its ability to engage users 

To gather feedback on the website's overall look and feel, as well as its usability

 

Testing Methods: 

The testing participants will consist of 3 expats (everyone did one expat) between the ages of 25 to 40 who are part of the target audience for the Xpat website. The test will be conducted remotely, and the participants will be asked to access the website on their personal computers.  

 The test protocol will include a set of tasks for the participants to complete, which will include navigating the website, making a donation, and exploring the content. Participants will be asked to think out loud and provide feedback as they interact with the website. The test session will be recorded for later analysis.

 

 Testing Results: 

Positive aspects of the UI/UX:

The website has a visually appealing design, with a modern look and feel 

The website is easy to navigate, with clear labels and intuitive menus 

The website provides users with a clear understanding of the brand's vision, mission, and values 

The website offers users option to donate/fund an support xpat

The website features engaging graphics and icons that enhance the user experience 

 

Negative aspects of the UI/UX:  

The content order needs improvement, with the donation button being more prominently displayed 

The "About Us" button in the navigation bar is too generic and should be renamed 

The website lacks interactive elements and could benefit from more hovers and carousels to increase user engagement Some tasks require too many inputs from the user, which slows down the process and creates confusion 

The website could benefit from a better navigation flow to improve efficiency  

 

Improvements Implemented: 

Based on the testing results, we would make several improvements 

 These include: 

Rearranging the order of content to display the service and donation button more prominently 

Renaming the "About Us" button to "Our Team" in the navigation bar 

Adding more interactive elements to increase user engagement, such as hovers and carousels 

Simplifying the user input process to enhance efficiency and reduce confusion Improving the navigation flow to make the website more user-friendly..  

 

Marketing

 

Context of campaign and promotional activities

The promotional activities we did include a social media campaign on Instagram @xpat.online. The Instagram account features posts and stories that showcase the platform's features. The posts feature a mix of content types like: tip of the week, quotes, fun fact, events, eventpictures. The Instagram account features user-generated content, which helps build credibility and authenticity for the platform. 

The objectives of the campaign, as outlined in the Communication Media Plan, include increasing brand awareness and engagement, promoting the platform's ease of use and benefits. 

The chosen platform Instagram, used for the campaign, were chosen to align with these objectives. Instagram is a popular social media platform with a large user base, particularly among the target audience for the Xpat platform. The visual nature of Instagram is also well-suited to showcasing the platform's features and benefits. Additionally, the use of user-generated content on Instagram helps to build trust and authenticity, which can encourage more users to engage with the platform.

 In conclusion, Xpat's campaign on Instagram is a well-executed promotional activity that aligns with the objectives outlined in the Communication Media Plan. The use of Instagram as a channel/platform allows Xpat to reach a large audience and showcase their platform's features and benefits through visual content and user-generated content.

 

Learning Points

Based on the Communication Media Plan and the analysis of the Xpat Online Instagram account, there are several learning points that can be identified:

 One important learning point is the effectiveness of social media platforms in reaching a target audience. The Xpat Online Instagram account has been successful in reaching its target audience through the use of engaging visuals and informative captions. The account has also utilized various promotional activities such as giveaways and partnerships to increase engagement and attract new followers. However, it was noticed that the account did not use all the recommended platforms in the Communication Media Plan, such as Facebook and Twitter. This suggests that the team could have reached a wider audience if they had utilized all the recommended platforms. 

Another learning point is the importance of consistent branding and messaging. The Xpat Online Instagram account has maintained a consistent visual identity through the use of a specific color scheme and branded graphics. The captions also consistently convey the message of the brand's mission to help expats in their new countries. This consistency has helped to establish a strong brand identity and create a sense of trust and familiarity with the audience. 

However, there were some aspects that did not work as expected. For example, while the account has a good engagement rate, the number of followers is relatively low. This suggests that while the account is effective at engaging with the existing audience, there is room for improvement in reaching and attracting new followers. Additionally, while the use of giveaways has been effective in increasing engagement, it is unclear if it has resulted in long-term engagement or conversions. 

If we were to do this project again, We would recommend exploring additional platforms, such as Facebook and Twitter, to reach a wider audience. Additionally, We would recommend exploring different types of promotional activities that can help to increase long-term engagement and conversions. Finally, We would suggest measuring the effectiveness of the promotional activities and adjusting the strategy accordingly to achieve the desired results.

Future Planning

Based on our experience working on the marketing campaign for Xpat.online, there are several recommendations we would make if the project were to continue: 

1. Increase engagement through interactive content: One area where the campaign could be improved is in the use of interactive content. While the current campaign features visually appealing graphics, there is room to incorporate interactive elements like quizzes, polls, and user-generated content that would increase engagement and encourage users to share the Xpat brand with their networks. 

2. Focus on building a stronger social media presence: While the Xpat Instagram account is visually appealing and features regular posts, there is room to increase engagement and followers. One way to achieve this could be to collaborate with influencers who align with Xpat's mission and values to reach a broader audience. 

3. Expand to other platforms: While Instagram is a valuable platform for engaging with users, there is room to expand Xpat's reach by creating a presence on other platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn. This would enable Xpat to reach users who may not be active on Instagram and provide more opportunities to engage with users across multiple touchpoints. 

4. Leverage user-generated content: As the campaign continues, there is an opportunity to engage with users and encourage them to share their own stories and experiences on the Xpat platform. This could be achieved through contests or social media challenges, where users are encouraged to share their own content using specific hashtags or by tagging the Xpat account. This would not only increase engagement but also provide valuable social proof for the brand. 

5. Continuously measure and analyze results: Finally, it is essential to continuously measure and analyze the results of the campaign to understand what is working and what can be improved. This could include analyzing engagement rates, conversion rates, and other metrics to identify areas for improvement and adjust the campaign accordingly. 

Overall, if the Xpat marketing campaign were to continue, it would be essential to focus on increasing engagement, expanding to other platforms, and leveraging user-generated content while continuously measuring and analyzing results to optimize the campaign for success.

Our team:

 

   Our team consists of three enthusiastic students ready to change the expat world. We are inspired by the braveness of expats and we believe they all deserve a safe space to come together, comfort each other and celebrate the great moments of life. For those who are starting an expat journey soon, it’s so good that you’re here and we can’t wait to make life changing memories with you all. We are thankful for every single one of you. 

Sending you our warmest regards, Xpat Team
